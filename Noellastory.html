The Irresistible Kitten

  • Introduction

    • There once was a child, oh so sweet,
    Who longed for a pet to call his own.
    He begged and he pleaded,
    "Please, let me keep,
    A kitten so fluffy, so adorable, and grown!"    >

  • Main Adventure

    • The kitten was tiny, with paws like soft puffs,
    A pink little nose, and big eyes like the moon.
    Its fur was so silky, so gentle to touch,
    This kitten was heaven, a precious boon!


    "Please, please, can I have it?" the child cried out.
    "Just look at its cuteness, its purr and its meow!
    I promise to love it and care for it well,
    Oh Mom and Dad, please say yes,
    I'll do it, I vow!"

  • Exciting Conclusion

    • The parents looked on at the kitten so small
    Its cuteness too much for any heart to ignore.
    They couldn't resist those pleading eyes after all,
    And so the child got the pet they'd been asking for.
